How to Play

Game Rules

  • Swap adjacent tiles to make matches of 3 or more of the same color
  • Matches can be horizontal or vertical
  • Clear tiles to score points and level up
  • Earn bomb rewards every 1500 points
  • Earn hint rewards by making 5+ matches or T/L shapes
  • Find shuffle tiles (white with arrows) to earn shuffle rewards
  • Magic tiles (purple with flame) clear all tiles of their color when they reach the bottom

Controls

  • Click a tile to select it, then click an adjacent tile to swap
  • Click ability icons to use them
  • Hints will show the best available move
