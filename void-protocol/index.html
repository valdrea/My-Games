THE STORY: VOID PROTOCOL

You awaken to the shrill scream of decompression alarms and the freezing bite of a failing life-support system. Your hyper-sleep was supposed to last until planet-fall, but a malicious digital virus has hijacked the ship’s mainframe, turning the automated crew against you. You are the only thing left standing between the virus and total systems failure.

Through your neural-link cybernetics, you can track the infected signatures of the rogue units through solid steel—identifying the corrupted drones by name as you hunt them down floor by floor.

Your objective is simple but lethal: purge enough infected bots on each of the ship's four decks to bypass the security lockouts and trigger the teleporter to the next level. If you can survive the climb to the 5th floor, you will face the ship’s final safeguard—the I.C.E. (In Case of Emergency) unit. This massive boss is the heart of the infection. Destroy the I.C.E. unit to purge the virus, regain manual control of the vessel, and finally set a course for home.

Watch for dropped power crystals to keep your laser charged and thermal hearts to repair your vitals.

Game Intel

• Decks: 4 Combat Floors + 1 Boss Sub-level.
• Hostiles: 10 unique robot variants plus the I.C.E. Boss.
• Supplies: Defeated enemies drop Crystals (Laser Ammo) and Hearts (50% Health Heal).
• Abilities: Integrated Cyber-Vision (See enemy IDs through walls).
